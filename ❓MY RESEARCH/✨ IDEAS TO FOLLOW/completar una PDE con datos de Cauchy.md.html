Complete a PDE with Cauchy data

Given a PDE of $k$-th order, we cannot down to $J^{k-1}E$ and define a distribution, unlike the case of an ode (same ODE in the two jet bundles). But, what if the initial-boundary conditions of a Cauchy problem just let us go down this way? I have tried with the wave equation (see [xournal 110] and [maple 018]).

That is, the initial-boundary conditions would let us construct additional PDEs that let us the visualization of DE systems in the same way that for ODEs.

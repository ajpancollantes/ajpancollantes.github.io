Visualization of DE systems

Given a system of DEs they are a subvariety of a jet bundle. For the simplest case of an ODE we can see ODE visualization in the two jet bundles. The idea would be to see the same for PDEs. But a first problem is that there can be two different solutions (prolongations of them) that have no empty intersection, see xournal_105.

Hay algo análogo al campo $A$ de las ODE pero para system of DEs de manera que la infinitesimal criterion of invariance se pueda plantear igual que$[X,A]=\mu A$ ????

Yes, but we have to include more PDEs. See xournal_111. This way, we can think of the intersection of the Cartan distribution $\mathcal{E}$ of $J^m E$ with the tangent bundle of the submanifold $S$ defined by the extended system of PDEs, which should has the same dimension of $J^{m-1}E$. Indeed, we would have an inclusion

$$ \iota: J^{m-1}E \rightarrow J^mE $$

being $\iota(J^{m-1}E)=S$.

And $\mathcal{E}_p\cap T_PS$ would define a distribution in $J^{m-1}E$ that would represent the equation.

Proposition: inverse relation

Let $\langle X_1,\ldots, X_{n-r}\rangle$ be a cinf-structure for the distribution $\mathcal{Z}$. Denote by $f_k$ a symmetrizing function of the vector field $X_k$ considered as a $\mathcal{C}^{\infty}$-symmetry of $\mathcal{S}(\mathcal{X}_k)$. Then $$\mu_k:=\frac{1}{f_k}$$ is an integrating factor for the $k$-th Pfaffian equation referenced in theorem integrability by Pfaffian equations

Special case: symmetrizing factors in the case of corank 1.

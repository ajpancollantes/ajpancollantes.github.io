Identification of vector bundles and its module of sections

See [Swan_1962, page 5, corollary 4].

Let $M$ be a normal topological space and $E, F$ two vector bundles over $M$. Then $E\approx F$ iff $\Gamma(M,E)\approx \Gamma(M,F)$.

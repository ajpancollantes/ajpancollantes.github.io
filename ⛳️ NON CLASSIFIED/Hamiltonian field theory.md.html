Hamiltonian field theory

In theoretical physics, Hamiltonian field theory is the field-theoretic analogue to classical Hamiltonian mechanics. It is a formalism in classical field theory alongside Lagrangian field theory.

Compared to it, in this case we introduce a function in the dual first order jet bundle $J^1(E)^{\dagger}$ , the Hamiltonian

$$ H:J^1(E)^{\dagger} \to \mathbb{R} $$

The solution of the system is given by the well-known Hamilton equations

$$ \frac{\partial H}{\partial u^{\alpha}}=-\frac{\partial p_{\alpha}^{i}}{\partial x^{i}} \quad \text { and } \quad \frac{\partial H}{\partial p_{\alpha}^{i}}=\frac{\partial u^{\alpha}}{\partial x^{i}} $$

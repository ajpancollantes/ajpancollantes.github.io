ODE

(particular case of system of DEs)

A $n$th-order ordinary differential equation is an expression

$$ u_n=\phi(x,u,\ldots,u_{n-1}) $$

It is a particular case of distribution. Debe verificar:

1. Es de rank 1.

2. Tiene lugar dentro de un jet bundle $J^{n-1}(\mathbb{R},\mathbb{R})$

3. Es peculiar en el sentido de que el vector que la genera es proporcional a $A=\partial x+u_1 \partial u+\ldots+u_{n-1}\partial u_{n-2}+\phi \partial u_{n-1}$

o equivalentemente la generan las 1-formas $\{\theta_0, \theta_1,\ldots,\theta_{m-2}, du_{n-1}-\phi dx\}$

siendo $\theta_i=du_i-u_{i+1}dx$

At the same time, it can be treated like a system of $n$ first order ODEs

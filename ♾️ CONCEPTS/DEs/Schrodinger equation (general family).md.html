Schrodinger equations

This is not a note for the original Schrodinger equation of Quantum Mechanics, but in the context of ODEs. They are equations in the form

$\frac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2} \Psi=g(x)\Psi$.

I found them first time appearing from a Riccati equation.

It is motivated by the time-independent Schrodinger equation in Quantum Mechanics, i.e., the search of the eigenstates of the Hamiltonian operator

$$ -\frac{\hbar^2}{2 m} \frac{d^2 \Psi}{d x^2}+u \Psi=E \Psi $$

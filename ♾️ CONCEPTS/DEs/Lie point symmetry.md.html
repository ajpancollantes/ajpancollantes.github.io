Lie point symmetry for an ODE

Es un caso particular de generalized symmetry of an ODE. En este caso

$$ Y=\xi(x,u) \partial x+\eta (x,u) \partial u + \sum_{i=1}^{m-1} \eta^{i}(x, u^{(m-1)}) \partial u_i $$

where $\xi,\eta \in \mathcal{C}^{\infty}(\mathbb{R}^2)$, y $\eta^{i}$ se ajusta a la misma fórmula de prolongación

$$\eta^{i}(x, u^{(m-1)})=A(\eta^{i-1})-A (\xi) \cdot u_i$$

que en el caso de las generalized symmetry of an ODE.

Lie point symmetry of a DE system

Is the 1-dimensional case of a symmetry group of a DE system.

Symplectic group action

It is a group action of a certain group $G$ over a symplectic manifold $(M,\omega)$ by means of symplectomorphism. They are also called groups of symplectic symmetries. The infinitesimal generators of this actions are symplectic vector fields.

Special case: Hamiltonian group actions

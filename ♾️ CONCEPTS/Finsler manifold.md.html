Finsler manifold

A Finsler manifold is a differentiable manifold $M$ together with a Finsler metric, which is a continuous nonnegative function $F: TM \to [0, +âˆž)$ defined on the tangent bundle so that for each point $x$ of $M$:

  • $F(v+w) \leq F(v)+F(w)$ for $v,w\in T_x M$.

  • $F(\lambda v)=\lambda F(v)$ for $\lambda \geq 0$.

  • $F(v)>0$ unless $v=0$.

See wikipedia.

Riemannian manifolds (but not pseudo-Riemannian manifolds) are special cases of Finsler manifolds.

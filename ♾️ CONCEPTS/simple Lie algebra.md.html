Simple Lie algebra

In algebra, a simple Lie algebra is a Lie algebra that is non-abelian and contains no nonzero proper ideals.

A simple Lie algebra cannot be a solvable Lie algebra.

A direct sum of simple Lie algebras is called a semisimple Lie algebra. A Lie algebra is semisimple if and only if its radical of a Lie algebra is 0.

A simple Lie group is a connected Lie group whose Lie algebra is simple.
